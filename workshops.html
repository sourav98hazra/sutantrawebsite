ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE (AI)

Artificial intelligence is the science of making machine do things that would require intelligence if done by men. It is an area of computer science that emphasizes the creation of intelligent machines that work and react like humans. Artificial intelligence must have access to objects,categories,properties and relations between all of them implement knowledge engineerng.Some of the activities computers with artificial intelligence are designed for include: speech recognition,learning,planning,problem solving.

DEEP LEARNING

Deep learning is a sub-field of machine learning concerned with algorithms inspired by structure and function of the brain called artificial neural networks. It encompasses machine learning, where machines can learn by experience and acquire skills without human involvement. It’s similar to how we learn from experience, the deep learning algorithm would perform a task repeatedly, each time tweaking it a little to improve the outcome.
