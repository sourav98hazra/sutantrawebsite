ABOUT

SUTANTRA-19, Department of Information Technology GITAM Institute of Technology

iTech observes the annual techno-management fest organized by the Department of Information Technology in GITAM (Deemed to be University), Visakhapatnam. iTech, in the year 2016 show cased the capabilities of the IT department. It has kick strated the "Programming Club".

This year, iTech is back on March 5th and 6th, 2019, taking a new shape and a new name "SUTANTRA 19". A team of final and pre-final year students of the Department of IT have worked hard to shape up this fest. 450+ students of all the years of IT Department have collectively worked to make this fest happen.
